About Me

I am a data scientist and AI researcher based in Bangalore, India, currently pioneering intelligent solutions at Fidelity Investments. My work spans vision transformers, uncertainty quantification, and large‑scale NLP systems. I have authored peer‑reviewed articles in journals such as Information Systems Frontiers and Knowledge Based Systems.

I thrive on transforming complex, uncertain data into actionable insight, with a focus on interpretable and resource‑efficient AI. My focus areas going forward are cognitive AI and visual context reasoning.

FGI‑CogViT: Fuzzy Granule‑based Interpretable Cognitive Vision Transformer

Information Systems Frontiers, 2024

A new vision transformer, namely Fuzzy Granule-based Interpretable Cognitive Vision Transformer (FGI-CogViT) which has three parts, namely feature extraction, fuzzy logic-based granulation, and I-CogViT-based classification. Various vision and statistical features are computed over the MRI scan image(s).

Real-time fall detection on road using transfer learning-based granulated Bi-LSTM

Knowledge-Based Systems, Elsevier

In this study, a new deep learning-based model, namely, transfer learning-based granulated Bi-LSTM (TLG-LSTM), is developed for fall detection on roads. The TLG-LSTM can handle the uncertainty issue arising between various ‘Fall’ and ‘No Fall’ events in complex scenarios concerning both indoor (i.e., home) and outdoor (i.e., road) areas.

NovaNet: A Novel Method for Enhanced Pothole Detection on Road

2024 8th International Conference on System Reliability and Safety (ICSRS)

Incorporating elements such as spatial awareness, gated convolutional operations, and attention mechanisms, NovaNet offers enhanced detection capabilities. Its multi-scale feature fusion block adeptly combines semantic details from various resolutions, ensuring comprehensive segmentation.
